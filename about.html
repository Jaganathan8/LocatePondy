About

Why LocatePondy exists, and how it works.

LocatePondy is a community directory of food shops across Puducherry — from the whitewashed lanes of White Town to the quiet backstreets of Ozhukarai. Every listing is organized by area, so you can explore your own neighborhood or a new one, one street at a time.
